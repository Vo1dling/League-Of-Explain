  One of the first things you learn in league of legends is the basic attack also known as auto attack and is
  something that is available to all characters/champions and in most situations cannot be avoided and deals
  damage based on your attack damage which can be increased through buying items in the shop   Like in most video games in league you have stats these stats range from: Increasing damage dealt with
  certain abilites, Taking reduced damage from a certain type, Healing yourself based on damage dealt etc....
  What differs from other games is that your stats reset at the start of each match
